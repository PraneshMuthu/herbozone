Research & Development

Pioneering Microbial and Bio-based Innovations for Agriculture, Aquaculture, and Poultry Systems
(Promoted by Bluezone Synthesis Private Limited)

Aquaculture
Research & Development

Probiotic Strain Development

  • • Isolation and formulation of Bacillus spp., Rhodobacter, and Rhodopseudomonas strains with high enzymatic activity and pathogen control.
  • • Emphasis on anaerobic strains to enhance sludge degradation and nitrogen cycle stabilization.

Bio-based Inputs

  • • Development of microbial consortia for reduction of organic load, ammonia, and hydrogen sulfide.
  • • Formulation of natural prebiotics and organic acids to support gut health in Penaeus vannamei.

Feed Additives & Health Solutions

  • • Creation of synbiotic feed additives combining spore-forming Bacillus strains with herbal actives.
  • • Execution of pilot trials focused on stress resistance, immune modulation, and hepatopancreas protection.

Agricultural
Research & Development

Microbial Biofertilizers

  • • Development of PGPR strains for nitrogen fixation, phosphate solubilization, and siderophore activity.
  • • Customized rhizosphere colonization strategies for varied agro-climatic soils.

Biostimulants & Crop Enhancers

  • • Formulation of fermented botanical extracts and microbial metabolites to promote plant growth and flowering.
  • • Incorporation of chelated amino acids and humic substances for improved nutrient uptake.

Biological Disease Management

  • • Biofungicide development targeting Fusarium, Rhizoctonia, Pythium, and various bacterial blights.
  • • Organic-compatible seed dressing and foliar treatment solutions.

Poultry
Research & Development

Probiotic Feed Supplements

  • • Multi-strain probiotic formulations with high acid and bile tolerance.
  • • Enzyme blends to improve cereal-based feed digestibility.

Antibiotic Growth Promoter (AGP) Alternatives

Development of phytobiotic-probiotic synergies to enhance immunity and intestinal health.

Pathogen Mitigation Strategies

  • • Natural antimicrobials effective against Salmonella, E. coli, and Clostridium species.
  • • Controlled trials on feed conversion, weight gain, and antibody response.

Mission G-BLUE

(Promoted by HERBOZONE)

The Earth's environment is confronting an unprecedented crisis marked by escalating pollution, climate change, and the depletion of the ozone layer. In response to these global challenges, MISSION G-BLUE emerges as a visionary scientific initiative and public awareness campaign.

This mission is designed to foster environmental responsibility and explore practical solutions for ozone preservation. The name embodies its purpose: "G" for a Green Earth and "Blue" for the ozone layer—both essential to sustaining life and planetary health.

MISSION G-BLUE integrates experimental research with community outreach to examine the impact of pollutants on ozone depletion and promote eco-friendly practices that support ozone recovery. Through this initiative, we aim to:

  • Emphasize the urgent need to reduce harmful emissions
  • Advocate for sustainable, science-based alternatives
  • Enhance public understanding of atmospheric science and its critical role in protecting the Earth

By combining scientific inquiry with environmental education, MISSION G-BLUE is not only a project of discovery but also a movement for action—empowering individuals and communities to join the collective effort to restore and protect our planet.

Products

Immunizer

Immunizer is a Enzyme and vitamin consortium product, Enzyme supplementation in poultry nutrition reduces feeding costs, allows flexibility on diets formulation, enhances performance and litter quality, improves poultry health, and reduces total production costs.

Benefits

  • improve nutrient digestibility and growth performance
  • It is added to soybean diet then improves weight gain and Feed: Gain ratio in broilers
  • Enzymes reduce viscosity, enhance gut transit rate.
  • decrease water amount in faeces, and provide a better litter quality

Proteinizer

Proteins that are involved in all anabolic and catabolic pathways of digestion and metabolism. Exogenous enzymes are administered in poultry diets to make diet formulation more flexible

Benefits

  • To reduce production cost
  • decrease digest a viscosity, to enhance nutrients digestion
  • To improve Apparent Metabolizable Energy value of the diet, to increase feed intake, weight gain.
  • To reduce beak impaction and vent plugging
  • To alter population of gut microorganisms, to lower water intake, water content of excreta
  • To improve the nutritional value of poultry diets and the subsequent performance
